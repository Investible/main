Glossary

Here we've assembled a list of terms that may be helpful for using our site.

Ticker Symbol

A ticker symbol is an arrangement of characters that represent a publicly traded company. Every ticker is unique and is used by investors to place trade orders.

Sales Revenue

Sales revenue represents the total income from the sale of goods and services. Sales revenue statistics have the associated costs subtracted ahead of time.

Earnings Per Share

A company's earnings per share is the portion of the company's profit that's allocated to each outstanding share. Earnings per share help indicate a company's profitability.

Assets

The word asset refers to any resource with economic value owned by an individual, company, or country.

Liabilities

Liabilities are a company's financial debts and obligations. Liabilities are settled over time through the transfer of money, goods, or services.
